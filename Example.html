BeaconPride is a growing Minecraft server, with a great community, and some awesome completely custom plugins!
Click here for join information!

Step 1 Download the Technic Launcher and run it. Login using your Mojang account when prompted.
Step 2 Click the + with Add New Pack below it and enter the pack URL: http://www.technicpack.net/api/modpack/beaconpride
Step 3 Add the pack and click Play to launch your game. The server IP will already be saved and once the game loads, just click the button labeled "Connect to BeaconPride" on the title screen to join!
